Canadian Pastry

This delicious Canadian pastry is a true delight. It's made with flaky layers of pastry dough and filled with sweet maple cream. A must-try for maple lovers!

Filipino Pastry

The Filipino pastry is a popular delicacy in the Philippines. It's a sweet and sticky treat made from glutinous rice flour and coconut milk. It's often topped with grated coconut or sesame seeds.
